The method used to calculate the carbon footprint is backed by science, but the calculated carbon footprint is only an approximate value because the exact carbon footprint depends on all of a user's actions and only a limited amount of information about each user is available to Us. Therefore, the calculation is made without any guarantee that your actual carbon footprint will correspond to your calculated carbon footprint. The method of calculation may be adjusted from time to time, for example because further findings are incorporated into the calculation methodology or because social conditions influencing your personal carbon footprint have changed.
